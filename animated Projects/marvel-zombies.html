Marvel Zombies

Release Date: September 24, 2025

Synopsis

The series follows a new group of survivors, including Spider-Man, Blade, and Kamala Khan, as they search for a cure to the zombie plague, traveling across a dystopian landscape and fighting super-powered zombies.
